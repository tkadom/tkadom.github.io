What is an Arduino?

  • Physical computing platform
  • USB programmable
  • Large Community
  • Multiple platforms
  • Based on ATmega328 - 8bit
  • 32k Memory
  • PWM, I2C, SPI
  • 14 Digital I/O ports (6 PWM outputs)
  • 6 analog inputs
